## Complete Quebec Incorporation Guide

### PHASE 1: Preparation (1-2 weeks)

#### 1.1 Choosing Legal Structure ⭐

**Available Options:**

**Sole Proprietorship** (Self-employed)

- Simple but offers no personal liability protection
- Not recommended for tech startups

**Corporation (Inc.)** - ⭐ **RECOMMENDED FOR YOU**

- Personal asset protection
- Credibility with investors
- Tax advantages (reduced rate 11.5% vs 53% personal)
- Cost: $335-500 (provincial incorporation) or $200 via Registrar (DIY)

**Why Corporation for QuestHunt?**

- Liability protection crucial when handling user data and physical locations
- Required for institutional investment rounds
- Professional image for B2B partnerships (tourism boards, municipalities)

#### 1.2 Name Verification ⚠️ CRITICAL

**Searches Required:**

1. **[Quebec Business Registry Search](https://www.registreentreprises.gouv.qc.ca/en/consulter/rechercher/)** (Registre des entreprises du Québec)
2. **[CBCA Database Search](https://ised-isde.canada.ca/cc/lgcy/fdrlCrpSrch.html)** (Canadian Business Corporations Act)
3. **[CIPO Trademark Search](https://ised-isde.canada.ca/site/canadian-intellectual-property-office/en/trademarks)** (if planning Canada-wide expansion)

**If Name Taken:**

- Use numbered company (e.g., 1234567 Canada Inc. doing business as "Forge Digital")
- Consider variant spellings or additions

#### 1.3 Documents to Prepare

- Government-issued photo ID (driver's license + health insurance card)
- Registered office address (can be home address)
- Business purpose (e.g., "software development, web and mobile applications, digital experiences")

#### 2.1 Provincial vs Federal Incorporation - ANALYSIS FOR QUESTHUNT

**🔍 RECOMMENDATION CHANGE: Federal Incorporation Now Recommended**

Based on QuestHunt's analysis, **federal incorporation is now the better choice** because:

**Provincial (Quebec) Pros:**

- Lower cost: $335 vs $200 (federal online) or $250 (federal certificate)
- Simpler if only operating in Quebec
- Faster processing (1-2 days)

**Federal (Canada) Pros - MORE RELEVANT FOR QUESTHUNT:**

- ✅ **Automatic name protection Canada-wide** (critical for scaling)
- ✅ **Easier expansion to other provinces** (Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary markets)
- ✅ **Better for attracting pan-Canadian investment** (VCs prefer federal corps)
- ✅ **Seamless integration with federal programs** (SR&ED tax credits, IRAP funding)
- ✅ **Professional credibility** for national tourism partnerships
- ✅ **QuestHunt has nationwide appeal** - Montreal is just the starting point
- Cost difference is minimal ($200 federal vs $335 provincial)

**Reality Check:**

- QuestHunt's value prop (geocaching + tourism) applies to ANY Canadian city
- Physical quests can be created nationwide by users (UGC model)
- Tourism board partnerships exist in every province
- No technical reason to limit to Quebec

**⭐ REVISED RECOMMENDATION: Federal Incorporation**

#### 2.2 Federal Incorporation Process (RECOMMENDED)

**Via Corporations Canada:**

- **Website**: [Corporations Canada Online Filing](https://ised-isde.canada.ca/cc/index.html)
- **Form**: Articles of Incorporation (Form 1)
- **Cost**: $200 (online) or $250 (certificate by mail)
- **Processing**: 1-2 business days (online), 7-10 days (mail)

**What to Define:**

- **Legal name**: "Forge Digital Inc."
- **Registered office**: Your Montreal address
- **Directors**: You (minimum 1, but 25% must be Canadian residents)
- **Share structure**:
  - Common shares: 100 shares issued (sufficient to start)
  - Value: $1/share = $100 initial capital (minimum)
- **Fiscal year end**: Recommended December 31

#### 2.3 Provincial Incorporation Process (Alternative)

**Via Quebec Business Registry:**

- **Website**: [Registre des entreprises du Québec](https://www.registreentreprises.gouv.qc.ca/en/demarrer/immatriculer/)
- **Form**: Registration of a legal person
- **Cost**: $335 (online)
- **Processing**: 1-2 business days

**What to Define:**

- **Legal name**: "Forge Digital Inc."
- **Head office**: Your Montreal address
- **Directors**: You (minimum 1)
- **Shareholders**: You (100% of shares initially)
- **Share structure**:
  - Common shares: 100 shares issued
  - Value: $1/share = $100 initial capital
- **Fiscal year end**: Recommended December 31

#### 2.4 Documents Received

**Federal Incorporation:**

- Federal Business Number (BN) - 9 digits
- Certificate of Incorporation
- Articles of Incorporation
- Must also register in Quebec for provincial tax (NEQ)

**Provincial Incorporation:**

- NEQ (Quebec Enterprise Number) - 10 digits
- Certificate of Constitution
- Articles of Incorporation

### PHASE 3: Tax Registrations (1 week)

#### 3.1 Federal Business Number (BN)

**Via Canada Revenue Agency (CRA):**

- **Website**: [CRA Business Registration](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/businesses/topics/registering-your-business.html)
- **Online Portal**: [My Business Account](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/e-services-businesses/business-account.html)
- **Cost**: Free
- **Processing**: Immediate (online)

**Accounts to Open Simultaneously:**

- ✅ **Payroll (RP)** - If paying yourself a salary (recommended for tax efficiency)
- ✅ **GST/HST (5%) + QST (9.975%)** - MANDATORY if revenues >$30K/year
- ⚠️ **Do NOT open** Import/Export account (not needed for digital products)

**Note for QuestHunt**: If offering premium subscriptions or B2B services, you'll hit $30K threshold quickly. Register for GST/QST from day one to avoid retroactive complications.

#### 3.2 Provincial Tax Number (QST)

**Via Revenu Québec:**

- Registers automatically when you register for GST via CRA
- Or separately: [Revenu Québec - Register for QST](https://www.revenuquebec.ca/en/businesses/consumption-taxes/gsthst-and-qst/registering-for-the-gsthst-and-the-qst/)
- **Cost**: Free

#### 3.3 Central Business Registry (CBR)

- Automatically created when registering for GST/QST
- Enables transactions with government entities
- Important for municipal partnerships (QuestHunt's B2B model)

### PHASE 5: Legal Documents & Governance (1-2 days)

#### 5.1 Corporate Minute Book ⭐ CRITICAL

**Mandatory Contents:**

- Articles of Incorporation
- Directors' register
- Shareholders' register
- Important resolutions:
  - Banking resolution
  - Share issuance
  - Director/officer appointments
  - Compensation resolution (your salary)

**Options:**

| Option                                                       | Cost       | Best For                                                                               |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **DIY**                                                      | $0-50      | Simple single-founder structure (use [Éducaloi templates](https://educaloi.qc.ca/en/)) |
| **Online service** ([LegalZoom](https://www.legalzoom.com/)) | ~$200      | Standard incorporation, some guidance needed                                           |
| **Notary/Lawyer**                                            | $500-1,000 | Complex structure, multiple founders, or investment rounds                             |

**Recommendation**: DIY initially, upgrade to lawyer when raising investment.

#### 5.2 Shareholders' Agreement (If future co-founders)

**When Needed:**

- Taking on co-founders or partners
- Before issuing shares to employees

**What It Covers:**

- Sale rights and restrictions
- Dilution protection
- Major decision-making authority
- Dispute resolution

**Cost**: $1,500-3,000 (lawyer/notary)
**Recommendation**: Not needed initially as sole founder, but essential before bringing on partners.

### PHASE 6: Annual Compliance

#### 6.1 Annual Obligations ⚠️ CRITICAL - DO NOT FORGET

**Annual Filing Requirements:**

| Requirement                        | Deadline                                   | Cost                     | Consequence if Missed              |
| ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| **Annual Return (Quebec)**         | Before incorporation anniversary           | $45/year                 | Dissolution, $100 reactivation fee |
| **T2 Tax Return (Federal)**        | 6 months after fiscal year end             | Accountant: $1,000-2,000 | Penalties, interest                |
| **CO-17 Tax Return (Quebec)**      | 6 months after fiscal year end             | Included with T2         | Penalties, interest                |
| **GST/QST Returns**                | Quarterly or annually (depends on revenue) | $0 (filing)              | Interest, penalties                |
| **T4/Relevé 1** (if paying salary) | February each year                         | $0 (filing)              | CRA/Revenu Québec penalties        |

#### 6.2 Accounting & Bookkeeping

**Years 1-2 (Revenue <$100K):**

- **Software**: [QuickBooks Online](https://quickbooks.intuit.com/ca/) ($20-70/month) or [Wave](https://www.waveapps.com/) (free)
- **Accountant**: For tax filing only - $1,000-2,000/year
- **DIY bookkeeping**: Track income/expenses monthly

**Years 3+ (Revenue >$100K):**

- **Accountant**: Monthly/quarterly - $200-500/month
- **Services**: Tax planning, salary vs dividends optimization
- **Audit preparation**: If seeking investment

**Recommendation for QuestHunt**: Start with Wave (free) + yearly accountant. Upgrade when revenue hits $100K or before fundraising.

#### 7.2 Insurance

**Professional Liability Insurance (E&O):**

- **Covers**: Software errors, data breaches, professional negligence
- **Cost**: $500-2,000/year (for $1-2M coverage)
- **Recommended for**: Health data apps (Cyclix, Intima), or apps handling sensitive personal data
- **For QuestHunt**: Consider when handling payment data or large user bases

**Cyber Liability Insurance:**

- **Covers**: Data breaches, cyberattacks, ransomware, legal costs
- **Cost**: $1,000-3,000/year
- **Essential if**: Processing payments (Stripe), storing user data, handling location data
- **For QuestHunt**: HIGHLY RECOMMENDED once you have 1,000+ users (location data + payments)

**Directors & Officers (D&O) Insurance:**

- **Covers**: Personal liability for board decisions
- **Cost**: $1,500-5,000/year
- **When needed**: After raising institutional investment
- **For QuestHunt**: Not needed until Series A funding

**Recommendation Timeline:**

- **Year 1**: Skip insurance, focus on product
- **Year 2** (>1,000 users): Add Cyber Liability
- **Year 3** (>$100K revenue): Add Professional Liability
- **Series A**: Add D&O Insurance

## ⚠️ Common Pitfalls to Avoid

| Mistake                                  | Consequence                                       | Prevention                                        |
| ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| ❌ **Mixing personal/business finances** | Lose legal protection ("piercing corporate veil") | Separate bank account, credit card, receipts      |
| ❌ **Forgetting annual Quebec return**   | Company dissolution, $100 reactivation fee        | Set calendar reminder 2 months before anniversary |
| ❌ **Not registering GST/QST at $30K**   | Retroactive penalties and interest                | Register proactively when approaching threshold   |
| ❌ **Not maintaining minute book**       | Problems when selling company or raising funds    | Update quarterly, document all major decisions    |
| ❌ **Ignoring tax planning**             | Pay too much tax (salary vs dividends)            | Consult accountant annually for optimization      |
| ❌ **Provincial when should be federal** | Costly later expansion, limited name protection   | Assess growth plans realistically upfront         |
